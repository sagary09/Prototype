Bradycardia

Adult Bradycardia-5021

Treatments per PA Statewide ALS Protocol

Consent- 1:00 PM Initial Assessment- 1:10 PM Vital Signs- 1:11 PM Arrived on scene and received consent to treat patient. Gathered initial assessment of patient. Took initial vital readings of patient. Blood pressure of patient was 158/96.

Pacing- 1:11 PM Initiated pacing at heart rate of 80 and 80 mAmps and obtained electrical capture.

12 Lead- 1:12 PM Oxygen- 1:13 PM Maintained patient’s airway. Administered Oxygen 15 lpm NR Mask. Monitored ECG bradycardia with third degree AV block. Pulse Oximetry 94% on room air Initiated IV 18g left A/C NSS hep-lock. 12-Lead ECG bradycardia with third detgree AV block. Patient has signs of poor perfusion.

Atropine- 1:15 PM Signs and symptoms of poor purfusion. Administered 0.5mg of Atropine with an IV.

Pacing- 1:19 PM Initiated pacing at heart rate of 80 and 80 mAmps and obtained electrical capture.

Medical Command- 1:20 PM Perfusion did not improve with pacing SBP < 100. Medical Command contacted to consider sedation options.

EPI-1:26 PM 2 doses of 10 mcg of EPINEPHrine administered with infusion.

Transport-1:30 PM Patient's heart rate improved with EPINEPHrine. Patient transported to Albert Einstein Medical Center.
